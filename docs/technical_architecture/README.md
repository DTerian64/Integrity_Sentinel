# Technical Architecture Objectives

The Integrity Sentinel technical architecture is designed to support:

- continuous integrity monitoring
- operational intelligence workflows
- explainable AI investigations
- relationship-centric analysis
- graph-assisted investigations
- tenant-scoped intelligence
- governed operational analytics
- enterprise SaaS scalability

The architecture intentionally combines:

- graph intelligence
- behavioral analytics
- AI orchestration
- operational workflows
- cloud-native infrastructure
- governed investigation models

---

# Architectural Principles

## Explainable Intelligence

The architecture prioritizes:

- explainable AI
- evidence traceability
- bounded retrieval
- explainable risk scoring
- attributable investigation context

The platform intentionally avoids opaque autonomous enforcement workflows.

---

## Human-in-the-Loop Investigations

AI systems are intended to assist investigators rather than replace them.

Human investigators remain authoritative for:

- escalation decisions
- compliance actions
- investigation conclusions
- governance workflows

---

## Tenant-Scoped Intelligence

All platform workflows are designed around:

- tenant-aware authorization
- tenant-scoped retrieval
- isolated investigation context
- governed graph traversal
- authorization-aware AI orchestration

---

## Cloud-Native SaaS Architecture

The platform is architected around:

- Azure-native services
- managed infrastructure
- scalable orchestration
- API-driven services
- operational observability
- distributed intelligence workflows
