- organizational relationships
- insider-risk indicators
- governance activities
- operational behaviors

The platform vision focuses on:

- operational trust
- explainable investigations

# Core Business Concepts

## Operational Integrity

Operational integrity refers to the continuous ability of an organization to:

- enforce governance policies
- maintain trustworthy workflows
- detect operational abuse
- identify suspicious behavior
- preserve accountability
- support auditable decision-making

---

## Relationship Intelligence

The platform emphasizes relationship-centric operational analysis across:

- employees
- approvals
- vendors
- transactions
- workflow systems
- operational evidence
- communication metadata

This allows the platform to identify hidden operational patterns and relationship-driven risks.

---

## Continuous Integrity Monitoring

Integrity Sentinel is designed around continuous operational monitoring instead of periodic auditing.

Potential monitoring domains include:

- procurement workflows
- approval chains
- insider-risk indicators
- behavioral anomalies
- governance drift
- operational relationship analysis

---

## AI-Assisted Investigations

The Sentinel AI Investigator is intended to support:

- investigators
- auditors
- compliance teams
- governance operations
- operational analysts

through:

- explainable summaries
- evidence correlation
- relationship discovery
- operational context analysis
- investigation workflow acceleration

Human investigators remain authoritative decision-makers.

# Governance Principles

The business architecture emphasizes:

- explainable investigations
- governed operational intelligence
- evidence traceability
- tenant-scoped intelligence
- authorization-aware workflows
- metadata-oriented relationship analysis
- human-in-the-loop oversight

The platform is not intended as an unrestricted surveillance system.
