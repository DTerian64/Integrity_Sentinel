## Architecture Page Copy

### Hero Headline

Governed intelligence architecture for enterprise integrity workflows.

### Supporting Copy

Integrity Sentinel is architected as a cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS platform that connects enterprise source systems to graph intelligence, behavioral analytics, AI investigation workflows, governed APIs, and evidence-backed case management.

### Architecture Principles

#### Explainable Intelligence

Risk scores, summaries, and investigation narratives should be traceable to evidence, relationships, and contributing factors.

#### Human-in-the-Loop Investigations

AI assists with context retrieval, evidence correlation, and narrative generation while human investigators remain responsible for conclusions and actions.

#### Tenant-Scoped Intelligence

Retrieval, graph traversal, authorization, investigation context, and evidence access are designed around tenant-aware boundaries.

#### Cloud-Native SaaS

The architecture aligns API-driven services, managed data stores, observability, identity, and scalable Azure-native infrastructure.

### Architecture Layer Copy

#### Enterprise Source Systems

Connect operational signals from HRIS, ERP, procurement, identity, collaboration metadata, and custom business applications.

#### Data Ingestion Layer

Normalize entities, relationships, approvals, transactions, evidence, and event streams for integrity analysis.

#### Integrity Intelligence Core

Analyze operational context using ontology modeling, graph intelligence, behavioral analytics, policy rules, anomaly detection, and evidence correlation.

#### AI Investigation Layer

Retrieve bounded context, explain risk factors, summarize anomalies, identify related entities, and draft investigation narratives.

#### Core Platform API

Coordinate tenant context, authorization, cases, alerts, entity graph access, and investigation workflows.

#### Investigation Workspace

Give investigators a governed interface for dashboards, case review, entity exploration, evidence analysis, and escalation workflows.

## Governance Page Copy

### Hero Headline

Explainable AI for governed investigations.

### Supporting Copy

Integrity Sentinel is designed to support operational governance and organizational trust while respecting enterprise boundaries. The platform emphasizes metadata-oriented analysis, configurable controls, evidence traceability, tenant isolation, and human oversight.

### Governance Commitments

- AI assists investigators rather than replacing them.
- Investigation context is scoped by tenant, authorization, and governance policy.
- Risk explanations are tied to evidence and contributing factors.
- Relationship intelligence focuses on operational metadata and enterprise workflows.
- Escalation decisions remain accountable to human reviewers.
- Audit logs and evidence retention support compliance workflows.

## Roadmap Page Copy

### Hero Headline

From concept architecture to enterprise integrity platform.

### Supporting Copy

Integrity Sentinel is currently in concept development, platform architecture modeling, SaaS planning, AI orchestration design, and workflow decomposition. The roadmap is structured to move from vision and architecture toward a believable prototype, operational MVP, domain modules, and enterprise maturity.

### Roadmap Stage Copy

#### Phase 0: Concept & Architecture Foundation

Define product vision, architecture boundaries, business capabilities, enterprise ontology, SaaS concepts, and AI investigation workflows.

#### Phase 1: Foundational Prototype

Build a minimal proof of concept with React, Vite, FastAPI, authentication, entity graph modeling, risk scoring, AI-generated summaries, and an investigation workspace.

#### Phase 2: Operational Intelligence MVP

Expand into multi-tenant SaaS architecture, RBAC, case management, audit logging, dashboards, tenant isolation, and AI-assisted investigations.

#### Phase 3: Domain-Specific Integrity Modules

Develop focused modules for HR integrity, procurement integrity, insider risk, compliance, audit readiness, and governance reporting.

#### Phase 4: Enterprise Platform Maturity

Advance toward production-grade SaaS capabilities such as advanced graph analytics, explainable AI workflows, policy simulation, predictive scoring, federated investigations, and executive dashboards.
