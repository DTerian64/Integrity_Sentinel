## Core Platform Modules

### Enterprise Integrity Graph

#### What It Does

Models operational entities and relationships across employees, managers, vendors, approvals, transactions, documents, devices, identities, evidence, and investigations.

#### Why It Matters

Risk often lives in relationships: repeated approvals, hidden vendor ties, unusual collaboration patterns, circular workflows, or transaction clusters that do not stand out in isolation. The Enterprise Integrity Graph gives investigators a connected view of operational context.

#### Example Signals

- Employee-to-vendor relationships
- Approval chains and override patterns
- Transaction clusters
- Shared identifiers or devices
- Related investigations and evidence
- Workflow proximity between entities

---

### Behavioral Risk Intelligence

#### What It Does

Establishes behavioral baselines and evaluates anomalies across approval patterns, spending trends, communication metadata, workflow behavior, semantic similarity, timing irregularities, and access activity.

#### Why It Matters

Integrity risk is rarely a single event. Behavioral analytics help the platform recognize unusual combinations of activity and explain which factors contributed to risk.

#### Example Signals

- Unusual approval frequency
- After-hours activity
- Duplicate or semantically similar requests
- Spending outside peer baselines
- Abnormal access behavior
- Workflow manipulation indicators

---

### Risk Scoring Engine

#### What It Does

Combines rules, behavioral analytics, anomaly detection, graph correlation, and evidence context into explainable risk scoring.

#### Why It Matters

Investigators need more than a score. They need to know why something was flagged, what evidence supports it, what relationships are involved, and what should be reviewed next.

#### Example Outputs

- Risk alerts
- Contributing factors
- Related entities
- Evidence links
- Suggested investigation paths
- Escalation recommendations

---

### Sentinel AI Investigator

#### What It Does

Assists investigators by retrieving bounded context, correlating evidence, summarizing anomalies, explaining risk factors, identifying related entities, and generating investigation narratives.

#### Why It Matters

AI is most useful when it reduces investigative burden while preserving accountability. Sentinel AI Investigator is designed to support human review, not replace human judgment.

#### Example Assistance

- Investigation summaries
- Executive narratives
- Anomaly explanations
- Related-entity discovery
- Evidence correlation
- Recommended next actions

---

### Investigation Workspace

#### What It Does

Provides a governed workspace for alerts, cases, entity exploration, evidence review, AI-generated summaries, investigator notes, escalation workflows, and audit archives.

#### Why It Matters

Integrity investigations need traceable decisions, clear evidence, controlled access, and repeatable workflows. The workspace is designed to help teams move from alert to review, escalation, resolution, and retention.

#### Example Workflow

1. Risk alert is created.
2. Context is retrieved from graph, evidence, and operational data.
3. AI generates an explanation and investigation narrative.
4. Investigator reviews the case and requests more context if needed.
5. Findings are escalated, resolved, or archived with supporting evidence.

---

### Governance & Administration

#### What It Does

Supports tenant context, authorization, role-based access controls, policy administration, auditability, retention controls, and governed intelligence workflows.

#### Why It Matters

Integrity intelligence must operate inside enterprise boundaries. The platform is designed for tenant-scoped retrieval, access-aware investigation context, and governance-aligned review.

#### Governance Principles

- Tenant-scoped intelligence
- Authorization-aware AI orchestration
- Evidence traceability
- Configurable controls
- Audit logging
- Human-in-the-loop review

## Data & Architecture

### Headline

Built around governed data flow from enterprise systems to investigation outcomes.

### Copy

Integrity Sentinel's architecture is designed around a clear operational flow: enterprise systems feed a data ingestion layer, data is normalized into the Integrity Intelligence Core, risk and relationship context flow into the AI Investigation Layer, and users work through a secure platform API and investigation workspace.

### Architecture Layers

- Enterprise source systems: HRIS, ERP, procurement, identity, collaboration metadata, and custom business applications.
- Data ingestion: connectors, batch import jobs, event streams, normalization, and mapping.
- Integrity Intelligence Core: ontology, graph database, risk scoring, policy rules, anomaly detection, and evidence correlation.
- AI Investigation Layer: Sentinel AI Investigator, narrative generation, and explainability services.
- Core Platform API: API gateway, authorization, tenant context, case management, alerting, and entity graph services.
- Data stores: operational database, graph database, evidence store, vector store, and immutable audit log.
- Security and governance: Microsoft Entra ID alignment, secrets management, observability, retention, and governance controls.
