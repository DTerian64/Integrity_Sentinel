## Problem Section

### Headline

Fraud, collusion, abuse, and governance drift rarely appear in one system.

### Copy

Operational integrity problems usually hide between systems: an approval pattern in one workflow, a vendor relationship in another, a timing anomaly in a transaction stream, or a behavioral shift that only becomes visible after the damage is done.

- Manual investigations struggle to correlate evidence across workflows.
- Periodic audits often discover patterns after they become systemic.
- Opaque AI is not enough for governance-sensitive decisions.

## How It Works

### Headline

From operational signals to explainable investigations.

### Workflow Copy

Integrity Sentinel is designed to connect enterprise source systems to a governed intelligence core. Data is ingested, normalized, scored, correlated through graph and evidence services, and routed into an AI-assisted investigation workflow.

### Workflow Steps

1. Enterprise source systems provide operational signals from HR, finance, procurement, identity, collaboration metadata, and custom business applications.
2. The ingestion layer normalizes entities, events, approvals, transactions, and relationship data.
3. The Integrity Intelligence Core applies graph analytics, behavioral models, anomaly detection, policy rules, and evidence correlation.
4. The Sentinel AI Investigator retrieves bounded context and generates explanations, summaries, and recommended next actions.
5. Human investigators review cases, request more context, escalate findings, and preserve evidence for auditability.
