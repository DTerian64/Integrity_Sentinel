# Integrity Sentinel Product Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Concept & Architecture Foundation

### Objectives

- Establish product vision
- Define architecture boundaries
- Model business capabilities
- Define enterprise ontology
- Build technical documentation
- Establish platform positioning

### Deliverables

- Product specification
- Business architecture diagrams
- Technical architecture diagrams
- Initial deployment architecture
- SaaS platform concept
- AI investigation workflow

### Current Status

IN PROGRESS

---

## Phase 1 — Foundational Prototype

### Objectives

Build a minimal but believable technical prototype.

### Proposed Scope

- React/Vite frontend
- FastAPI backend
- Basic authentication
- Entity graph model
- Risk scoring prototype
- AI-generated investigation summary
- Simple investigation workspace

### Proposed Capabilities

- Entity ingestion
- Relationship visualization
- Semantic similarity detection
- Simple anomaly scoring
- AI narrative generation
- Alert creation workflow

### Target Outcome

Demonstrable proof-of-concept platform.

---

## Phase 2 — Operational Intelligence MVP

### Objectives

Transform prototype into a minimal operational platform.

### Proposed Scope

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Case management workflows
- RBAC model
- Tenant isolation
- Audit logging
- Dashboarding
- AI-assisted investigations

### Potential Integrations

- Microsoft Entra ID
- Azure OpenAI
- Azure SQL
- Cosmos DB
- HR systems
- Procurement systems

### Target Outcome

Early-stage enterprise demo environment.

---

## Phase 3 — Domain-Specific Integrity Modules

### Potential Modules

#### Sentinel HR Integrity

Detect:
- favoritism
- nomination rings
- approval abuse
- suspicious reward distributions

#### Sentinel Procurement

Detect:
- duplicate invoices
- suspicious vendor relationships
- procurement collusion
- approval bypassing

#### Sentinel Insider Risk

Detect:
- abnormal access behavior
- privilege misuse
- after-hours anomalies
- workflow manipulation

#### Sentinel Compliance

Support:
- audit readiness
- governance reporting
- policy monitoring
- evidence retention

### Target Outcome

Modular enterprise integrity platform.

---

## Phase 4 — Enterprise Platform Maturity

### Objectives

Expand toward enterprise operational intelligence platform maturity.

### Potential Capabilities

- advanced graph analytics
- explainable AI workflows
- policy simulation
- predictive risk scoring
- federated investigation workflows
- cross-tenant analytics models
- executive intelligence dashboards

### Target Outcome

Production-grade enterprise SaaS platform.
