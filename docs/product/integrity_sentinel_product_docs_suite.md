# Integrity Sentinel
## AI-Powered Enterprise Integrity Intelligence

**Continuous detection of fraud, collusion, abuse, insider risk, and operational anomalies through behavioral analytics, relationship intelligence, and AI-driven investigations.**

---

# Executive Summary

Integrity Sentinel is a conceptual enterprise intelligence platform designed to help organizations continuously monitor operational integrity across people, workflows, approvals, vendors, transactions, and operational systems.

The platform combines:

- graph analytics
- behavioral modeling
- explainable AI
- anomaly detection
- relationship intelligence
- AI-assisted investigations
- operational workflows

into a unified enterprise integrity platform.

Integrity Sentinel is intended to help organizations move from reactive auditing and periodic investigations toward continuous operational trust monitoring.

---

# Platform Vision

Integrity Sentinel is envisioned as an enterprise integrity intelligence system capable of:

- detecting fraud and collusion
- surfacing hidden operational relationships
- monitoring behavioral anomalies
- assisting investigators with AI-generated context
- supporting governance and compliance operations
- continuously evaluating organizational risk

The platform focuses on operational integrity rather than traditional perimeter cybersecurity.

---

# Strategic Positioning

Integrity Sentinel is positioned as:

> A Palantir-inspired operational intelligence platform for organizational integrity and enterprise trust.

The platform concept sits between:

- operational intelligence
- fraud analytics
- insider risk monitoring
- behavioral analytics
- AI-assisted investigations
- governance intelligence

rather than functioning as a traditional SIEM or generic analytics dashboard.

# Core Platform Concepts

## Enterprise Integrity Graph

The Enterprise Integrity Graph models:

- employees
- managers
- vendors
- approvals
- transactions
- documents
- devices
- identities
- investigations

and continuously analyzes relationships between entities.

---

## Behavioral Risk Intelligence

The platform establishes behavioral baselines and continuously evaluates:

- approval patterns
- spending trends
- communication relationships
- workflow anomalies
- semantic similarity
- timing irregularities
- access behaviors

---

## AI Investigation Assistance

The Sentinel AI Investigator assists analysts by:

- correlating evidence
- summarizing anomalies
- generating investigation narratives
- identifying related entities
- explaining risk factors
- recommending next actions

---

## Continuous Integrity Monitoring

Integrity Sentinel is designed around continuous operational monitoring instead of periodic auditing.

The platform continuously evaluates:

- fraud indicators
- collusion networks
- insider-risk signals
- policy violations
- governance drift
- workflow manipulation
