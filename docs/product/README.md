# Product Vision

Integrity Sentinel is envisioned as a modern enterprise intelligence platform capable of continuously evaluating operational integrity across:

- people
- workflows
- approvals
- vendors
- transactions
- operational systems
- relationship networks

The platform combines:

- graph intelligence
- behavioral analytics
- explainable AI
- operational workflows
- cloud-native SaaS architecture

to support AI-assisted investigations and operational trust monitoring.

---

# Strategic Positioning

Integrity Sentinel is positioned between:

- operational intelligence
- fraud analytics
- insider risk monitoring
- behavioral analytics
- governance intelligence
- AI-assisted investigations

The platform is not intended to function as:

- a traditional SIEM
- a generic BI dashboard
- a standalone cybersecurity platform
- an unrestricted surveillance system

Instead, the platform focuses on:

- operational integrity
- explainable investigations
- relationship-centric intelligence
- governance-aligned analytics
- metadata-oriented operational analysis
